# Developer Fitness Plan

## Monday
- Walk to coffee machine (3 reps)
- Carry laptop to different room for "change of scenery"
- Aggressive typing (cardio)

## Tuesday
- Stand up for standup meeting
- Sit back down
- Rest day

## Wednesday
- Pace nervously during production deployment
- Stress-induced sweating (sauna alternative)

## Thursday
- Running... the build
- Jumping... to conclusions
- Pushing... to main

## Friday
- Sprint retrospective (it's in the name)

## Weekend
- Recovery period
- Carb loading (pizza)

Remember: consistency is key. Do these exercises every week and you'll definitely be a developer.
