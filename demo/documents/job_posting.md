# Job Posting: Senior Junior Developer

## About Us
We're a fast-paced startup that moves fast and breaks things (mostly our own things, accidentally).

## Requirements
- 10+ years of experience with technologies that came out 2 years ago
- Expert in all programming languages (yes, all of them)
- Ability to work under pressure (constant, unrelenting pressure)
- Must be passionate about our mission (we haven't decided what it is yet)
- Rock star ninja guru wizard (we use all the buzzwords)

## Responsibilities
- Write clean, maintainable code under impossible deadlines
- Attend meetings about planning meetings
- Estimate tasks (we will then cut your estimate in half)
- Be on call (always)
- Mentor junior developers (who make more money than you)

## Benefits
- Competitive salary (we compete with ourselves to pay the least)
- Unlimited PTO (that you'll feel guilty for using)
- Free snacks (it's just a bowl of fun-size candy bars)
- Flexible hours (you can choose which 12 hours per day to work)
- Equity (0.0001% that will definitely be worth millions someday)

## Interview Process
1. Phone screen (30 min)
2. Technical assessment (8 hours)
3. On-site interview (full day)
4. Take-home project (we might use this in production)
5. Final interview with CEO
6. Please complete this 3-month trial period unpaid

Apply now! We're like a family (dysfunctional and you can't leave).
