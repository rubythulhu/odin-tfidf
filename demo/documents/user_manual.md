# User Manual: Smart Coffee Maker X9000

## Safety Information
Do not submerge in water. It's a coffee maker, why would you do that.

## Getting Started

1. Unbox your Smart Coffee Maker X9000
2. Remove all packaging materials (save the box, you'll need it for the return)
3. Plug into electrical outlet
4. Download the companion app (requires account creation)
5. Grant permissions: camera, microphone, location, contacts, your firstborn
6. Connect to WiFi (will disconnect randomly)
7. Update firmware (30 minutes)
8. Update firmware again (you thought once was enough?)
9. Create coffee profile
10. Link social media accounts (why? don't ask)
11. Subscribe to Coffee+ Premium for basic features

## Troubleshooting

**Problem**: Coffee maker won't turn on
**Solution**: Is it plugged in?

**Problem**: App won't connect
**Solution**: Restart phone, restart coffee maker, restart router, restart life

**Problem**: Coffee tastes bad
**Solution**: Buy better coffee (not covered under warranty)

**Problem**: Machine asking for password reset
**Solution**: Your coffee maker has been hacked. Make coffee manually like a caveperson.
