# World's Okayest Banana Bread

## Ingredients
- 3 overripe bananas (the blacker, the better)
- 1.5 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- Optional: chocolate chips (mandatory)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Or 175°C if you're fancy.
2. Mash those bananas like they owe you money.
3. Mix in the butter, egg, and sugar.
4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until combined.
5. Pour into greased loaf pan.
6. Bake for 60 minutes or until your smoke alarm doesn't go off.
7. Let cool before slicing, or don't. I'm not your mom.

Serves: 8-10 people or 1 person over several days of questionable choices.
